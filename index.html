About Me

I am a research assistant in the Stevens Center for Innovation in Finance at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, advised by Prof. David Musto and Prof. Brett Hemenway Falk. I am broadly interested in the blockchain technology, in particular, decentralized finance (DeFi). Currently, I am working on NFT wash trading by developing and implementing filters that correctly flag trading anomalies.

This May, I earned my MA in Applied Mathematics (AMCS) from the University of Pennsylvania. During my studies, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Brett Hemenway Falk and Prof. Gerry Tsoukalas.

Prior to Penn, I obtained my MA in Visual Arts Administration from New York University under the guidance of Prof. Amy Whitaker, and BS in Mathematics from Union College, NY, with departmental honors, summa cum laude, and Phi Beta Kappa.

Feel free to reach out if would like to learn more about my research or talk about film photography.
