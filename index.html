About Me

I am an incoming PhD student in Operations Management (DOTM) at UCLA Anderson School of Management.

Previously, I worked as a research assistant at the University of Pennsylvania's Crypto and Society Lab (CASL), mentored by Prof. Brett Hemenway Falk and Prof. Gerry Tsoukalas.

I am broadly interested in algorithmic game theory, particularly in addressing challenges arising from new technologies and digital platforms. Recently, I have been working on Decentralized Finance (DeFi). My work in this area, especially concerning Non-Fungible Token (NFT) wash trading and royalties, contributes to better market designs that enhance regulatory practices and empower creators.

Feel free to reach out if you would like to learn more about my research or talk about film photography.
